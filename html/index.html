At Sparkline Decor we have a variety of handcrafted resin

Epoxy Resin is a versatile material that's used in a wide variety of crafts. Resin casting is a fun way to accent your furniture, create home decor items, etc. Resin starts as a liquid and then hardens, so you can pour it into molds, or add items like dried flowers, colors and glitters.
At Sparkline Decor we create Trays, Platters, Wallclocks, Tables, Coasters, Napkin Holders, Cake and Cupcake stands and many more at reasonable prices. We also provide an option to customize the products based on client requiremnets.
